# 🚀 Launching org-v2: AI-Native Organization with 8.4x Efficiency

**Hook**: Built an AI-native organization system from scratch in 5 hours. The result? 8.4x faster capability growth than existing frameworks.

---

## The Problem

When AI agents work in an organization, they solve tasks but lose knowledge.

- Most frameworks: 14% of work becomes reusable capabilities
- Result: Every similar task starts from zero
- Cost: Wasted time, repeated mistakes, slow growth

**Example**: org-blueprint completed 14 tasks → 2 reusable skills (14% rate)

---

## The Solution: org-v2

A minimal AI-native organization framework with **mandatory capability deposition**.

**Core mechanism**: Every task ends with "Can this be reused?"
- Not optional → Mandatory
- Result: 117% deposition rate (7 capabilities from 6 tasks)
- **8.4x faster** than existing frameworks

---

## What Makes It Different

### 1. Extreme Simplicity
- 3 layers (vs 5 in others)
